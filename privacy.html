Privacy Policy – Ringly

Last updated: 1st January 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Voxstream Technologies Pvt. Ltd. (“VTPL”, “we”, “us”, “our”) collects, uses, discloses and protects your information when you use the ringly mobile application (“App”) and the website ringly.in (“Website”). The App and Website together are referred to as the Platform. By using the Platform, you acknowledge that you have read, understood and agreed to this Privacy Policy and consent to the handling of your information as described.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

Account Information: Name, username, email, phone number, date of birth, gender (optional).
Content: Photos, videos, audio, comments, messages and interactions you share through Ringly.
Verification Data: Identification details needed for authentication or security checks.
Feedback / Support Queries: Any content you share with our support or via surveys.

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

Device Information: Model, OS, language, time zone, browser type.
Usage Information: Features used, time spent, interactions, preferences, session logs.
Location Data: Approximate location based on your device settings.
Log Data: IP address, crash reports and diagnostic data.

1.3 Information From Third Parties

Data shared by service providers, advertisers, analytics partners and other users interacting with your content.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Create and maintain your account
  • Provide, personalise, and improve Platform features
  • Recommend content and enhance discovery
  • Ensure safety, integrity, and compliance
  • Prevent fraud, abuse, and harmful behaviour
  • Provide customer support
  • Conduct analytics and research
  • Fulfil legal or regulatory obligations

3. Sharing of Your Information

3.1 With Service Providers

With trusted third-party vendors who assist in hosting, analytics, security, communication delivery and customer support.

3.2 With Other Users

Content you share publicly may be visible to other users.

3.3 Within Our Corporate Group

Within Voxstream Technologies Pvt. Ltd. and its affiliates for operational purposes.

3.4 For Legal Reasons

We may disclose information if required to comply with law, regulation or governmental request, respond to legal process, or protect rights, safety or property of users or VTPL.

3.5 In Business Transfers

In events such as mergers, acquisitions or restructuring, relevant data may be transferred.

4. Storage of Your Information

Your data is stored securely with Google Cloud Platform on servers located in India. We use appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data. However, no system is entirely immune from security risks.

5. Your Rights

You may have rights to access, correct, delete your data, withdraw consent, or request a copy by contacting support@ringly.in.

6. Data Retention

We retain your information as long as your account is active, as necessary for purposes outlined in this policy, and as required under law. Certain logs or aggregated data may remain stored after deletion for compliance and security.

7. Third-Party Links & Embeds

The Platform may display links or content from third-party websites or services. We are not responsible for their privacy practices. Please read their Privacy Policies before interacting with them.

8. Communications

We may use your information to send service updates, account notifications, security alerts, and promotional communications (with consent). You can opt out from promotional messages at any time.

9. Children’s Privacy

The Platform is not intended for individuals under 13 years of age (or as defined under applicable law). We do not knowingly collect personal data from children without appropriate parental consent.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Policy periodically. Continued use of the Platform after updates constitutes acceptance of the revised Policy. Significant changes will be notified within the Platform or via email.
