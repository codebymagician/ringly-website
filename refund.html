Refund & Cancellation Policy

At Ringly, we strive to provide the best experience to our users. Please read our refund and cancellation policy carefully.

Refund Policy

For any refund request, please drop us an email at support@ringly.in.

Please mention your registered phone number in the email for faster resolution.

Cancellation Policy

If you wish to cancel any service or booking, please contact us at support@ringly.in with your phone number and relevant details.

Processing Time

Once we receive your request, our team will review it and get back to you within a reasonable timeframe. Refunds, if applicable, will be processed as per our internal review and payment partner timelines.
