Refund & Cancellation Policy

This Refund & Cancellation Policy outlines the terms and conditions for payments made on the Ringly mobile application, operated by Voxstream Technologies Private Limited.

1. Nature of Services

Ringly provides digital services, including real-time 1:1 audio conversations. Since these services are consumed instantly (per-minute billing) or involve the transfer of digital goods, they are generally non-refundable once utilized.

2. Virtual Credits / Coins

Users purchase digital credits/coins to access premium audio features.

  • Non-Refundable: Once coins have been used to initiate a call, the transaction is considered final and cannot be reversed.
  • Unused Balances: If you have an unused credit balance and wish to seek a refund due to a technical error, please contact our support team within 48 hours of the transaction.
  • Refunds will not be issued for minutes already consumed during a conversation.
  • In the event of a documented technical failure (e.g., a call drop due to a platform server error where the user was billed for minutes not provided), Ringly will credit the affected minutes back to the user’s in-app wallet upon verification.

4. Technical Malfunctions

If a payment is successful but the credits fail to reflect in your account due to a technical glitch:

Please email us at support@ringly.in with your Transaction ID and Registered Mobile Number. We will investigate and either credit the amount to your wallet or initiate a refund to the original payment source within 5-7 working days.

5. Cancellation Policy

Users can stop using the service at any time by ending the audio call or closing the application. There are no long-term subscription commitments or cancellation fees. Once a call is ended, no further per-minute charges will be accrued.

6. Abuse of Policy

Voxstream Technologies Private Limited reserves the right to refuse refund requests if there is evidence of fraud, platform abuse, or violation of our Terms of Service.
